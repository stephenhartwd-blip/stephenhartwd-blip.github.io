Questions

Is Whale Tracker free?

Yes — downloading and browsing recent sightings is free. Whale Tracker Pro unlocks real-time alerts, full map history and migration routes.

Where do the sightings come from?

From a trusted network of spotters, whale-watch operators, and research feeds worldwide — checked and de-duplicated before they hit your map. Dead or stranded animals are filtered out.

Does it work for whale-watching tours?

That's exactly what it's for. Check the live map before booking a tour or picking a lookout — whales spotted this morning are usually still in the area.

Which devices does it support?

iPhone and iPad, on the App Store today.

Do you really track sharks now?

Yes — great white shark sightings now appear alongside whales, so surfers and swimmers can check the water too.
